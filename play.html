Chess Learning Resources

Basic Chess Rules

  • The board consists of 64 squares.
  • Pawns move forward, but capture diagonally.
  • The king moves one square in any direction.

Common Chess Terminology

Term Description
Check When the king is under threat of capture.
Checkmate When the king is in check and cannot escape.
En passant A special pawn capture under certain conditions.
